\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements a general physical-\/type neuron. 

The neuron membrane\textquotesingle{}s current and synaptic currents are descibed by a current contribution in which a saturating charge-\/up function is multiplied by a discharge function In the corresponding states, the neuron is in one of its Gen\+Comp\+Stage\+Machine\+Type\+\_\+t states
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Gen\+Comp\+Stage\+Machine\+Type\+\_\+t\+::gcsm\+\_\+\+Computing computing (collects charge)
\item gcsm\+\_\+\+Delivering\+: delivers action potential
\item gcsm\+\_\+\+Relaxing\+: restores resting potential
\end{DoxyItemize}

The Heartbeat\+\_\+method() handles an internal \textquotesingle{}state update\textquotesingle{} signal. In the corresponding stages, the neuron is
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item gcsm\+\_\+\+Relaxing\+: Until it reaches its resting potential, calls \doxyref{Calculate\+\_\+\+Do()}{p.}{classNeuronPhysical_a4de3701160dfcbc4cd2a60e8c8d01eef}. Upon receiving an input, changes to stage gcsm\+\_\+\+Processing. Inputs enabled\+: the relative refractory period.
\item gcsm\+\_\+\+Computing\+: Until it reaches its threshold potential, calls Calculate\+\_\+method(). At that point, mebrane passes to stage gcsm\+\_\+\+Delivering. Inputs enabled.
\item gcsm\+\_\+\+Delivering\+: Until it reaches the threshold membrane potential again calls Calculate\+\_\+method(). (The neuron delivers its result to its output section.) After terminating, it passes to stage gcsm\+\_\+\+Relaxing, without restoring membrane\textquotesingle{}s potential. At the beginning \textquotesingle{}Transmitting\+Begin\textquotesingle{} is also issued. (Sends \textquotesingle{}Begin Transmitting\+Begin\textquotesingle{}) \begin{DoxySeeAlso}{See also}
\doxyref{sc\+Gen\+Comp\+\_\+\+PU\+\_\+\+Abstract}{p.}{classscGenComp__PU__Abstract}.
\end{DoxySeeAlso}
Signal transmission happens in parallel with the further processing. Inputs disabled; the absolute refractory period.
\end{DoxyItemize}
